Pacific Cod & Orange Fennel Salad

Ingredients:

• 4 skinless Pacific cod fillets
• 2 tablespoons of olive oil
• 2 teaspoons of lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
• 2 fennel bulbs, cored and thinly sliced
• ¼ teaspoon of salt
• ¼ teaspoon black pepper
• 2 oranges, segmented
• 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

2. In a shallow dish, stir together the olive oil, lemon juice and garlic powder.

3. Place the cod fillets in the olive oil mixture and coat them well. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and let the cod marinate for 10 minutes.

4. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine the fennel, salt, and pepper.

5. Place the cod fillets in a shallow baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes, until the cod is cooked through.

6. Add the orange segments and parsley to the bowl with the fennel and toss to combine.

7. When the cod is cooked through, divide among four plates and top with the orange fennel salad. Serve immediately.
